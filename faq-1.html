Asked Questions

If your question hasn't been addressed in our FAQ or if you need more detailed assistance, please don’t hesitate to get in touch with us.

Do you provide 100% Placement Guarantee?
While we cannot give fake promises, we offer robust support services such as resume assistance, interview preparation, and networking opportunities to enhance your chances of securing employment post-training. Your job placement will depend on the progression of your skillset development and alignment with industry requirements, ensuring opportunities align with your growing capabilities. Our focus is on equipping you with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in your chosen field.
Is EMI Option available?
We offer Divided Payment options to make our courses more accessible which is you can pay half amount while joining the Course and pay the next half amount after 30 days. We understand the financial commitments of pursuing education. We are working on to provide EMI option. Feel free to reach out to our admissions team for further details and assistance with payment plans.
Will one to one Mentor support be provided?
Our programs include one to one mentor support to help you with any challenges you face during the course. Our mentors are industry experts who can provide personalized guidance to ensure you get the most out of your training.
Where will I be able to access Video classes?
All our video classes are hosted on our secure online learning platform, accessible to registered students 24/7. Upon enrollment, you will receive login credentials to access the platform from anywhere with an internet connection, allowing you to learn at your own pace and convenience.
How many hours do I have to spend every day for this course?
The time commitment varies depending on the course and your individual learning pace. We recommend dedicating 1hour or sufficient time each day to engage with the course materials, practice exercises, and participate in any interactive sessions or discussions. Our courses are designed to be flexible to accommodate diverse schedules and commitments.
